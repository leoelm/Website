How Abode Works.

Simple. Quick. Convenient.

Download.

Set yourself up for a new future. Our App will be available for both, iOS and Android. Our team is working day and night to make a launch possible by October 2020. Stay updated by signing up to our waitlist.

Sign Up.

Enter your details and set your preferences. That's it. We will make sure to provide you with houses that fit your needs and wishes.

Swipe.

Swipe left and right in order to save the houses that you like. Go through your recommendations and find your perfect fit. We are sure you will get the hang of it quite easily.

Compare.

Check out all the houses you have liked and compare them side by side. This will give you a better idea of which house might be the one for you.

Book.

No more need to find the phone number or email of the person responsible for the propterty. Just stay on the app and use the integrated chat in order to communicate with the propterty manager and set up your viewing.
